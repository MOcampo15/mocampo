Why is Software Engineering Important? 

         Software engineers take on the important job of creating programs and they, 
         along with other teams and professionals, 
         take on the important task of making things work smoothly for its users. 
         Although software engineering is fairly new, it's one of the fastest 
         growing branches of engineering because new technology is being developed
         on the daily and can be used on a variety of platforms.

Uses:

  • Mobile:Creation of software for mobile applications.
  • Robotics: Create and develop software needed for control of robots and automation.
  • Cloud Computing: Create, manage and design software that will be needed in cloud computing systems.
  • Health Informatics: Creation of software that will be used in health care and public health organizations to organize patient information.
  • Security: Analyses computer systems and makes sure that they are working properly and creates software that prepares computer systems for any future issues such as cyber attacks.

Among many more uses!
